ARC@National Univ. of Singapore

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Advanced Robotics Centre at National University of Singapore, supervised by Professor Marcelo Ang. My currently research interests include comprehensive video understanding, video based perception, self-supervised learning, and multi-modal learning.

Currently, I am a visiting student in the Mars Lab at Tsinghua University, supervised by Professor Zhao Hang, working at Qi Zhi Institute. The main research topic is video based environment perception. I am fortunate to have conducted research in the Vision Lab at DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group, under Professor Rong Jin, and Vision4Robotics Group at Tongji University under Professor Changhong Fu, where I spent wonderful times.
